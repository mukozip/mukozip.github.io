Dairy Management App

Enter cow details to track daily AM (Morning Milk) and PM (Evening Milk) production. After adding a cow, input milk values for each session. The app calculates totals, deductions, and lactation periods based on the current date (August 14, 2025). Data is saved locally and persists across page refreshes. Delete cows for specific months without affecting other months. Export to CSV for Excel use or export/import JSON for cross-device transfer.
